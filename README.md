# TRLAFCO App 🍶
A Flutter-based logistics and management application for TRLAFCO dairy operations.

---

## Project Background

This project is a mobile app creation using flutter inspired by the capstone project, Analytics-Driven Supply Chain and Inventory Management System developed for The Rosario Livestock and Agriculture Farming Cooperative. TRLAFCO was an agricultutral cooperative located in Rosario, Batangas. Established in 2012 and currently has around 115 registered members, 79 of whom actively deliver raw milk.

---

## Features

- **Logistics Dashboard**: View daily deliveries, pending classifications, and log new milk shipments.
- **Manager Dashboard**: Tracks overall analytics, milk volume charts, and displays financial payouts.
---

## About the Project

TRLAFCO currently records milk deliveries, quality classifications, farmer-supplier information, and payment data through a mix of paper logs and disconnected spreadsheets. Delivery volumes are written down by hand, then transcribed again by different staff members into separate files with no links between them. This creates duplicate entries, inconsistent figures, and no single reliable source of truth for day-to-day operations.
This application centralizes those records into one system, giving Logistics Staff a way to log deliveries and classifications directly, and giving the Manager a consolidated view of inventory and payouts without manually re-keying data from multiple files.

### Target Users

- **Manager** – oversees end-to-end operations: daily delivery totals, raw milk inventory, farmer-supplier payment computation, receipt generation and analytics.
- **Logistics Staff** – receives and weighs raw milk from farmer-suppliers, classifies deliveries as Class A or Class B, and logs the results.

---

## Modules

1. **Delivery and Classification Module** – lets Logistics Staff record raw milk deliveries and assign grades through a trackable pending-to-history workflow.
2. **Farmer-Supplier Records Module** – registration, viewing, and status tracking of farmer-supplier information.
3. **Manager Dashboard and Records Module** – consolidates delivery, inventory, and payment data into a single role-based interface.
4. **Analytics and Insights Module** – visualizes milk supply trends and generates descriptive insights to support inventory planning.
